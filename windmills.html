Bangui Windmills

Bangui Wind Farm is a wind farm in the Philippines, in Bangui, Ilocos Norte. Over the years, these windmills have developed into a global tourist attraction that, like the famous Don Quixote, has inevitably attracted and anticipated millions of visitors.

How to get to Bangui Windmills, Ilocos Norte from Manila

  • The Northwind Bangui Bay Project is situated at the northwest tip of the island of Luzon, in the municipality of Bangui, Ilocos Norte, Philippines
  • If you're going to drive from Laoag City, take the Pan-Philippine Highway / AH26. You'll be able to see Cape Bojeador and Wind Farm Burgos.
  • There are also direct bus ride from Manila to Agno, Pangasinan although it is very limited.
  • After passing the Burgos Wind Farm, turn left for a few metres. Travel straight ahead until you enter Estress Resort, then turn right to arrive at the destination.

Activities to do in Agno Bangui Windmills

  • Sightseeing
  • Picture Taking

When is the best time to visit?

Bangui Windmill Farm is visited by thousands of tourists, particularly during summer season. There are no costs to pay to get a beautiful shot beneath one of the giant windmills. To get a view of the stunning windmill draw, make sure to visit the windmill farm during the daytime.
