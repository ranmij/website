Imugan Falls

Imugan Falls is a 35-foot single layer cascade along Imugan River which is located in the village of the same name (Imugan). Its cold water emanates from a spring atop Mount Imugan dropping to a shallow basin before flowing downstream.
The waterfalls is approximately 1.5 kilometers from its trailhead at the main road of Barangay Imugan and can be reached in about 30 minutes of leisurely walk along a paved trail dotted with signages and directions. With its grandeur and natural beauty, Imugan Falls is worthy adventure. Its accessibility makes it a favorite stop for tourists who wish to experience the Nueva Vizcaya.

How to get to Imugan Falls from Manila

  • From Manila catch a bus to Isabela or Cagayan and get off at Santa Fe, Nueva Vizcaya
  • From the highway, transfer to a tricycle to Barangay Imugan

Activities to do in Imugan Falls

  • Swimming
  • Picture Taking
  • Sightseeing

When is the best time to visit?

During Summer or Sunny day is the best day to visit, explore and experience the beauty of the falls, It is not advisable to visit to the waterfalls during rainy days.
